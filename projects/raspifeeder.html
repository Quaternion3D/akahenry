Raspberry Pi Pet Feeder

My mom complained that it was a hassle to come home in the middle of the day every day just to feed our dog, so I designed and built an automatic pet feeding machine using Python and a Raspberry Pi. I also used a framework called NetIO to design a mobile app to control the feeder. Through the app, users can set a timer for when they want the feeder to dispense food.
